---Defeat the Beast!---

A random number will be displayed at the start of the game.

There are four weapons below. By clicking on a weapon you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You defeat the beast and win by matching your total score to the Number of the Beast. You are eaten by the beast and lose the game if your total score goes above the Number of the Beast.

The value of each weapon is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each weapon.
